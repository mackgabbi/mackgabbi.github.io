Personal Statement:

. . .I hate the world, but I love the world. I know everything and I know nothing. I despise affection, yet I love affection. These are my reasons for joining this site. I often crave affection, of my own want and need. I am searching for someone who will allow me to both control them and make them inferior to me. You may have to deal with my rants about how much I hate human existence, but you will learn to love me despite my differences. . . I am pleading with anyone who reads my profile to take into consideration my desperate need for love, because I need something different, quite different, for which I am thirsting, but which I cannot find!
My philosophy is that we can truly love only with suffering and through suffering! We know not how to love otherwise. Without suffering, there is no other feeling. We know no other love. I want suffering in order to love. I want and thirst this very minute to kiss, with tears streaming down my cheeks, this one and only I have left behind. I don't want and won't accept any other. Know that I grew up without a home; and perhaps that's why I've turned so . . . unfeeling, I implore you to help an intelligent and well-read man with the most prominent void within his life.
I hope you all can take into consideration the great service you will be doing both to yourself and myself by involving yourself with me. All in all, I believe that with love one can live even without happiness, for where there is love, there are never shadows too dark to overcome.
